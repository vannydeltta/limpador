SCHEMA: AutomaticPayment
TYPE: object

PROPERTIES:
- request_id (string): ID do pedido
- cleaner_email (string): Email da faxineira
- amount (number): Valor a ser pago para a faxineira
- pix_key (string): Chave PIX da faxineira
- status (string): enum [pending, processing, completed, failed], default: pending
- payment_proof_url (string): URL do comprovante
- payment_date (string): Data e hora do pagamento (formato: date-time)
- error_message (string): Mensagem de erro, se houver

REQUIRED FIELDS:
- request_id
- cleaner_email
- amount
- pix_key
